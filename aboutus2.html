JanaMitra - A platform for a better tomorrow 

We are dedicated to fostering a community-driven approach to waste management and environmental sustainability. Our platform serves as a hub for individuals to come together, report observed waste-related issues, and share innovative ideas to enhance our public spaces.

We are a group of dreamers with a vision in our minds. We hope to bring together smart technology and environmental sustainability to create a community in which every individual can take care of their surroundings. And we are out looking for such dreamers to help us build a better world.

To support global decarbonization efforts, our 500-MW manufacturing facility for hydrogen power sources stands ready to deliver technologically advanced solutions thriving in unique and demanding deployment contexts.

Our comprehensive solutions encompass Thyristorised and Switch mode rectifiers tailored for various industrial applications, thriving in unique and demanding deployment contexts. Emphasizing resilient power electronics, each rectifier guarantees reliability and efficiency, featuring attributes like High Efficiency, multi-pulse system, Low ripple, High Power Factor, and Low Total Harmonic Distortion.

Notably, ADOR heavy-duty rectifiers power electrolytic cells for hydrogen production through electrolysis. We supply DC Power sources and rectifiers to electrolyser manufacturers and hydrogen producers, ensuring the efficient operation of hydrogen-generating cells.

Our systems meet stringent specifications in the electrolytic water hydrogen production industry, available in 6, 12, and 24 pulse systems. ADOR’s thyristor rectifiers adhere to international standards, including CE certification, with design capabilities encompassing automatic power factor and harmonic mitigation. Additionally, our state-of-the-art switch mode technology offers scalability, user-friendliness, and minimal maintenance requirements, ensuring continued operation and cost savings.

JanaMitra is overseen by a group of six students from PCET’s PCCoE. We are a group whose mindset is fashioned by engineering for the sake of development. With our heads constantly brainstorming for new ideas, it is necessary for us to look out for the impact of our work on our environments, and whether it is compatible with the principles of sustainable development. Sometimes, it is more convenient to search for ideas whose goals are to restore nature, and if possible, contribute to the holistic development of our society.

JanaMitra: Harnessing Action for Real-Time Waste Management

The idea of JanaMitra, especially that of its unique functioning, was derived from the necessity for immediate action to real-time problems. Through the lens of statistics, it is observed that even though improper waste disposal on a larger scale follows certain patterns, they are only emergent, and that on the scale of a locality, the waste-distribution is random and unpredictable. Drops come together to form waves, and waves come together to form the sea, it is known. JanaMitra thus focuses on such localities to keep this improper waste disposal at bay.
